    Principal's Message

    Words of wisdom from our visionary leader

    Dr. D. Deva

    M.A., M.Phil., Ph.D.

    Founded in 1881, by the initiatives of the visionary first principal, Rev. G.T. Washburn, the American College began with only four Christian young men. Since then the institution has grown phenomenally in academic excellence and student enrollment...

    The College continues to attract students by offering courses that are globally relevant and equipping them with in-demand employability skills. Our students have consistently excelled in academics, sports, and cultural activities at national and international levels.

    We take pride in our state-of-the-art infrastructure, dedicated faculty, and a curriculum that blends traditional values with modern technology. The college has been ranked 59th among colleges in India in NIRF 2025.

    I invite all aspiring students to join this prestigious institution and be part of a legacy that has been shaping futures for over 14 decades. Together, let's build a brighter tomorrow.

    Dr. D. Deva
    Principal, Syed Ammal College
